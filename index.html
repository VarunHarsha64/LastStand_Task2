Last Stand

zombies vs YOU

Game Features

Dual Gun Modes:

Sniper Mode: High damage with heavy recoil and slower reload.

AR Mode: Moderate damage with fast recoil and quick reload.

Player Mobility: Move left, right, and jump to dodge attacks and gain strategic advantage.

Increasing Challenge: Zombies attack from both sides, with numbers growing each level.

Variety of Zombies:

Fast Attackers: Swift and aggressive.

Slow Walkers: Steady but dangerous in numbers.

Power-Ups

For every 10 kills, receive a random power-up:

Immunity: Temporary invincibility.

Faster Reload: Quicker reload speed.

Mega Bullet: A powerful shot that annihilates all zombies in its path until it hits a wall.
